Q: What is the difference between Brightspace and MyPurdue?
A: Brightspace is for daily coursework (assignments, quizzes, lecture slides), while MyPurdue is for administrative tasks (final grades, course registration, financial aid, and transcripts).

Q: Are there keyboard shortcuts?
A: Yes! Press / to jump to the search bar, Esc to clear it, or numbers 1-9 to instantly open the corresponding search result.

Q: How do I find my Academic Advisor?
A: Check the "Academic Profile" card or log into BoilerConnect to schedule an appointment.

Q: Where is my class schedule?
A: Your full schedule is in MyPurdue under Registration.
